Ministry of Culture

The Ministry of Culture, Government of India, is responsible for the preservation, promotion and dissemination of art and culture across the nation. It works towards safeguarding India's tangible and intangible heritage, supporting artists, strengthening institutions and enabling cultural participation at all levels.
